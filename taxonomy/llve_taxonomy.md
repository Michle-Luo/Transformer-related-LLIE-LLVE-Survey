# LLVE taxonomy notes

LLVE requires temporal consistency and motion-aware modeling in addition to frame-level quality improvement.

- **Alignment-aware spatio-temporal attention**: These methods use explicit alignment with spatio-temporal attention to reduce motion-related artifacts across frames. See [LLVE methods](../papers/video_methods.md).
- **Implicit spatio-temporal attention**: These methods model temporal relationships without explicit alignment modules, often through implicit sequence interactions. See [LLVE methods](../papers/video_methods.md).
- **Event-guided cross-modal spatio-temporal attention**: These methods use event or auxiliary cross-modal signals to support temporal reconstruction under low-light conditions. See [LLVE methods](../papers/video_methods.md).
